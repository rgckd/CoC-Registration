Circles of Connection (CoC)

What is CoC?

A self-study group consisting of 5 to 8 members willing to come together every week for 90 minutes to learn and practice Heartful Communication skills over 20 weeks typically.

Who can participate?

Any and all Heartfulness practitioners.

Program Structure & Duration

Over 20 weeks

90 minutes to practice Heartful Communication tools and techniques in a group, consisting of 5 to 8 members.

Languages offered

English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada through the registration form.

Also English, French and German outside India through a special registration process via email.

Process of registration 

◾ A study group consists of 5-8 members.

◾ Each member who wishes to be part of a CoC can register by filling out this form: Register Now

◾ Once registered, you will receive a short orientation video introducing the CoC process. We request you to watch the video before joining your study group, as it will help you understand how the CoC works and make your experience more meaningful.

◾ One member from each group will be designated as the Group Coordinator. The Coordinator will create a WhatsApp group to facilitate communication among the members.

◾ Learning materials will be shared with all registered members.

◾ The study group will begin meeting weekly after members have watched the orientation video and are familiar with the CoC process.

Learning objectives for participants

◾ To explore basic aspects of HC.

◾ To learn respectfully and fruitfully together as peers. [the coordinator has a practical role only and has no more authority than any other group member].

◾ To practice how to implement learning into their lives.

Materials provided

◾ Study material and weekly session guidelines.

◾ Participants can use these guidelines to conduct their own sessions.

◾ It is recommended that participants who are fluent in English purchase the following books:

Dr.Marshall Rosenberg: Nonviolent Communication: A Language of Life

Lucy Leu: Nonviolent Communication Companion Workbook (optional)

Extended learning through the "Giraffe" programme.

◾ Once in every 6-8 weeks an interactive session known as Giraffe Session is organized for all CoC groups together. All the CoC group members are welcome to join these sessions. 

◾ These sessions enable different CoCs  to come together for sharing and co-learning.

◾ These sessions are facilitated by a HC mentor. There are different Giraffe sessions for English, Hindi and other languages.

After completing the study material for over 20 weeks, the group can continue as a practice group
